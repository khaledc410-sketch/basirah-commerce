# Vizby — raw research notes

**Captured:** 2026-07-16  
**Method:** in-app browser review of the Shopify listing, official website, product gallery, pricing, and public reviews.

## Sources

- Shopify listing: https://apps.shopify.com/ai-blog-post-generator-1
- Homepage: https://www.vizby.ai/

## Marketplace snapshot

- Built for Shopify.
- Shopify rating: 5.0/5 from 30 reviews in the reviewed listing.
- Launched: 23 October 2025.
- 14-day trial.
- Essential $29/month: three visibility tests, Gemini, ten prompts, twenty product audits, five blog posts, three action plans, and two AGENTS.md outputs.
- Business $125/month: eight tests, Gemini and ChatGPT, twenty prompts, one hundred audits, twenty blogs, and collection support.
- Growth $249/month: three models including Perplexity, thirty-five prompts, seven hundred audits, and fifty blogs.
- Scale $499/month: four models including Claude, fifty prompts, two thousand audits, and two hundred blogs.

## Landing page and marketing observations

- Primary promise: “Become the Brand AI Recommends.”
- The page uses a deep forest-green palette, editorial serif/sans typography, generous spacing, and a premium Shopify-native feel.
- Two paths are visible: direct Shopify installation and a demo.
- The vendor says it is trusted by 500+ brands; this is a self-reported figure.
- The story is unusually clear: understand visibility, receive prioritized actions, implement in Shopify, then measure sessions, conversions, and revenue.
- A case-study claim shown on the site describes a 91.3% increase in AI sessions over two weeks and 3% of revenue from AI sessions. Treat this as a vendor case study, not a general expected outcome.

## Product flow and report observations

1. Run prompt tests across supported answer engines and competitors.
2. View platform tabs, the number of topics in which the brand appeared, a score, citations, and a query-level table.
3. Receive prioritized on-site, catalog, and off-site actions.
4. Open a task, inspect the proposed content or structured-data change, and approve, revise, reject, or save it to Shopify.
5. Track past actions, queued work, auto-published changes, and pending approvals in an activity log.
6. Re-run tests and connect activity to traffic and commercial outcomes.

The reviewed gallery showed:

- a query dashboard with platform tabs, topic coverage, citations, and prompt-level status;
- an activity log with auto-approval and queued tasks;
- an approval workflow for content opportunities with reject/revise/save controls;
- a product-audit table with per-product scores and actions such as fixing JSON-LD or enhancing content.

## Product scope

- AI visibility tests across several models depending on plan.
- Product and collection audits.
- JSON-LD, `llms.txt`, blog, FAQ, and catalog remediation.
- Off-site actions presented around Reddit, PR/citations, and LinkedIn.
- Approval and auto-application modes.

## Evidence cautions

- The app is relatively new and the marketplace proof base is still small.
- Some reviewed ratings came from merchants with short usage windows.
- Claims that an app can directly control recommendations, citations, Reddit presence, or revenue need to be separated from what it can actually publish or measure.
- Broad write permissions and auto-approval make versioning, diff review, and rollback critical product requirements.
