# Chatty homepage — raw research notes

**Pulled:** 2026-07-15  
**Source:** https://chatty.net/

- Self-reported scale: 20,000 customers/stores served.
- Positioning: AI sales agent for ecommerce rather than a support-only chatbot.
- Primary motion: free entry, automatic Shopify catalog synchronization, then usage-based paid upgrades.
- Named social proof includes Decathlon, ALDO, Gallery Furniture, Accurascale, and other ecommerce brands.
- Setup promise: connect the Shopify store, synchronize the catalog, enable the widget, and begin serving shoppers.

These are company claims, not audited financial or active-paying-customer figures.
