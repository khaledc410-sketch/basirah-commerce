## Evidence-backed sequence

1. The product launched on Shopify in June 2022.
2. Its initial wedge was simpler than the current AI product: WhatsApp contact, live chat, FAQ/help center, and order tracking.
3. During 2024 it expanded operational capabilities such as deep links, conversation history, FAQ tools, team assignment, and inbox features.
4. During 2025 it added Instagram connectivity, FAQ analytics, and broader merchant workflow functionality.
5. In January 2026 it migrated to chatty.net and sharpened its positioning around AI-assisted ecommerce sales.
6. In 2026 it added catalog-aware recommendations, homepage AI assistance, response-source review, and Shopify order-level revenue attribution.
7. By July 2026 it self-reported 20,000+ stores served, 50 million questions answered, and more than $1 million in additional sales attributed to chat.

The sequence is supported by public product pages and release notes. The customer and sales totals are self-reported and do not establish the number of current paying subscribers.
