# Chatty Shopify reviews — raw research notes

**Pulled:** 2026-07-15  
**Source:** https://apps.shopify.com/chatty/reviews

- Snapshot: approximately 1,804 reviews and a 4.9/5 overall rating.
- Frequent praise themes: responsive human support, hands-on setup assistance, ease of use, FAQ automation, and reduced repetitive support work.
- Common friction themes visible in lower-rated reviews: occasional reliability issues, setup complexity, customization conflicts, and concern about pricing changes.
- Multiple reviews describe support staff directly configuring or troubleshooting merchant stores. This suggests high-touch onboarding and customer success contributed materially to adoption.

No review excerpts are reproduced here; notes are paraphrased for research use.
