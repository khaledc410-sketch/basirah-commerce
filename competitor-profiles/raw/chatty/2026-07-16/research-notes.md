# Chatty — supplementary raw research notes

**Captured:** 2026-07-16  
**Method:** in-app browser review of the current Shopify listing, official homepage, interactive industry demo, product imagery, pricing, and public reviews. This supplements the 2026-07-15 raw notes.

- Homepage: https://chatty.net/

- Fashion demo: https://chatty.net/demo/fashion/

## Current snapshot

- Built for Shopify.
- The opened listing showed 4.9/5 from 1,691 reviews. Cached/search snapshots observed during research showed about 1,800; use “roughly 1.7k–1.8k reviews” in narrative comparisons unless rechecking at publication time.
- Launched: June 2022.
- Free tier; Basic $19.99/month; Pro $68.99; Plus $199; seven-day trial; $0.40 per extra AI conversation was shown in current pricing details.
- Scope includes AI answers, live chat, FAQ, order tracking, proactive engagement, cart recovery, mobile apps, and an omnichannel inbox covering channels such as email, Instagram, WhatsApp, and Messenger.

## Landing page and marketing observations

- Primary promise: one AI agent for every customer, question, and sale.
- The design is approachable rather than enterprise-heavy: yellow/indigo gradient, playful badges, centered hero, bold rounded typography, and clear free/demo calls to action.
- Marketplace validation and customer scale appear close to the main promise.
- The site reports 20,000 customers. Other self-reported numbers varied by page: the homepage showed more than $1M in assisted revenue while the demo used a much larger chat-revenue figure. The definitions may differ, but the inconsistency should be resolved before relying on either number.

## Demo flow

1. Choose an industry: fashion, furniture, electronics, beauty, or sports.
2. Enter a realistic storefront for that industry.
3. Open the widget and select from tailored buying questions.
4. Experience product discovery in context before installing the app.

The fashion demo opened with four category-relevant prompts. This turns a generic “book a demo” CTA into a self-guided product experience and reduces the imagination required from a merchant.

## Review themes

- Strong recurring positives: responsive support, ease of installation, breadth of support/commerce features, and value at the lower tiers.
- Negative themes observed across public feedback: price sensitivity, reliability or login friction, and occasional FAQ/app-embed issues.
- Strategic pattern: Chatty first won a familiar support job, then expanded toward recommendations, proactive selling, attribution, and omnichannel operations.
