**Captured:** 2026-07-16  
**Method:** in-app browser review of the Shopify listing, official landing pages, pricing, product screenshots, and public Shopify reviews.

## Marketplace snapshot

- Shopify rating: 4.7/5 from 109 reviews in the reviewed listing.
- Launched: 18 April 2022.
- Free installation path and a 14-day trial.
- Listing tiers observed: free for 100 visitors; Starter $104/month for up to 10,000 visitors; Basic $209; Standard $368; $12 per additional 1,000 visitors.
- The official site showed different starting-package numbers: Sales Agent $99, AI Concierge $118, and Support Agent $49. Treat channel pricing as a current inconsistency, not one definitive price table.
- Listed integrations include Shopify, Gorgias, Klaviyo, Zendesk, Attentive, Postscript, and Tapcart. The official site claims 175+ native integrations.

## Landing page and marketing observations

- Category frame: an “Agentic Commerce Operating System,” not merely a chatbot.
- Hero combines a high-fashion editorial photograph, cream/black palette, oversized copy, and two conversion paths: start a trial or book a demo.
- Risk reversal appears immediately: no credit card, fast installation, a trial, and a self-reported ROI guarantee.
- Merchandising is outcome-led: conversion, average order value, support deflection, and customer intelligence.
- Claims shown on the site include 10–30% conversion lift, 50–70% fewer tickets, 16%+ AOV lift, 97% of questions answered, and 5x ROI. These are vendor claims, not independently verified figures.
- Social proof and trust badges are placed close to the hero rather than hidden in a case-study section.

## Product flow and report observations

1. Install and synchronize the catalog.
2. Configure visual design, tone, knowledge sources, and “skills” or power-ups.
3. The agent detects behavioral intent and can initiate a conversation rather than waiting for a typed question.
4. It guides discovery, compares products, recommends items, supports checkout, or hands a conversation to a person.
5. The analytics dashboard reports assisted sales, conversion rate, product additions to cart, and live conversations.
6. Rep Insights categorizes conversation evidence into themes such as price objections, fit confusion, competitor mentions, unanswered questions, and missing information.
7. Shopper intelligence can be synchronized to marketing tools such as Klaviyo.

The reviewed product gallery showed a visually polished dashboard with assisted-sales and conversion metrics; a brand setup flow; knowledge uploads/links; catalog synchronization; configurable skills; and a live preview.

## Review themes

- Positive: fast setup, responsive human support, useful recommendations, accuracy after configuration, and integrations.
- Friction: one reviewer described spending one or two days correcting responses before the agent was ready.
- Product lesson: the onboarding/success team is part of the perceived product, especially when AI quality needs calibration.

## Evidence cautions

- Website performance and ROI numbers are self-reported.
- Visitor-based pricing can be harder to forecast than conversation- or outcome-based pricing.
- The pricing difference between the Shopify listing and the official site should be rechecked before any public comparison is published.
