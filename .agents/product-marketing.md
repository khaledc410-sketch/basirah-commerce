# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-07-17*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** بصيرة turns customer questions into safer product recommendations, merchant intelligence, and evidence-led AI visibility improvements for Arabic commerce.
**What it does:** Basirah connects to a Salla catalog, helps shoppers choose from verified products, and organizes conversations into needs, objections, demand gaps, and content opportunities. A free public website report measures technical, content, entity, SEO, AEO, and GEO readiness with page-level evidence and a 30/60/90-day improvement plan.
**Product category:** Arabic AI sales agent, customer intelligence, and AI-search visibility platform.
**Product type:** B2B SaaS for e-commerce merchants, with a free lead-generation report.
**Business model:** Free website report; paid implementation and recurring merchant plans after Salla marketplace and billing approval.

## Target Audience
**Target companies:** Saudi and Gulf e-commerce merchants, starting with Salla stores; strongest fit for product catalogs where customers need help choosing.
**Decision-makers:** Store owner, e-commerce manager, growth/marketing lead, customer-experience lead.
**Primary use case:** Help shoppers choose confidently while showing the merchant what customers want, what blocks purchase, and what content or catalog action should come next.
**Jobs to be done:**
- Recommend suitable in-stock products from verified catalog data.
- Convert conversations into prioritized customer needs, objections, and demand gaps.
- Diagnose whether a store is understandable to Google and AI answer systems, with evidence and a repair plan.
- Turn unanswered buying questions into reviewable Arabic content opportunities.
**Use cases:**
- A shopper states a need, constraint, and budget in Arabic; Basirah filters the catalog before explaining a recommendation.
- A merchant reviews recurring objections and unmet needs instead of reading conversations one by one.
- A prospect enters a public store domain and receives a free evidence-led visibility report before connecting Salla.

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Merchants have customer questions, catalog data, and website content, but those sources do not form one decision loop.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Generic chatbots answer fluently without grounding every price, stock state, or product fact.
- Dashboards report activity without explaining the next merchant action.
- SEO or “AI visibility” tools often blur technical readiness, discovery, and observed mentions.
- Agencies can produce reports, but the report is disconnected from daily customer demand.
**What it costs them:** Missed purchases, repetitive support work, weak product pages, and content that does not answer real buying questions.
**Emotional tension:** “I have data everywhere, but I still do not know what customers need or what to fix first.”

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** AI shopping assistants and Salla chat apps — can fall short when recommendations are not grounded in synchronized price, stock, attributes, and safety rules.
**Secondary:** SEO/GEO audit products — can fall short when readiness scores are detached from customer conversations and merchant execution.
**Indirect:** Manual support plus spreadsheets and agencies — can fall short on continuity, prioritization, and speed.

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- One loop: shopper question → verified recommendation → structured signal → content/catalog opportunity → measured readiness improvement.
- Arabic-first recommendations that understand need, budget, and dialect.
- Deterministic filtering before language generation; missing facts remain missing.
- Readiness, Google discovery, and observed AI visibility are separate measurements.
- A free, evidence-led public report creates value before OAuth or account creation.
**How we do it differently:** Basirah starts from verified catalog and page evidence, then uses AI to explain and organize—not to invent the underlying facts.
**Why that's better:** Merchants can trust the recommendation and act on the insight.
**Why customers choose us:** They get both a customer-facing sales agent and a merchant-facing improvement system built for Arabic commerce.

## Objections
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| “Will the agent invent a product, price, or stock level?” | Candidate products are filtered from synchronized catalog records first. Missing facts are disclosed or handed off. |
| “Can you guarantee that ChatGPT or Google will mention my store?” | No. Basirah measures verifiable readiness and keeps it separate from discovery and observed visibility. |
| “Do you need my Salla password?” | No. The integration uses Salla OAuth and requests only the scopes needed for the enabled release. |
| “Is the free report a sales teaser?” | The unlocked report includes all findings, page evidence, a prioritized plan, and an executive PDF; implementation is the paid service. |

**Anti-persona:** A merchant seeking guaranteed rankings, fabricated growth claims, unrestricted automated publishing, or recommendations unsupported by catalog facts.

## Switching Dynamics
**Push:** Repetitive support, low-confidence chatbots, disconnected analytics, and vague optimization reports.
**Pull:** A free evidence-led report, Arabic catalog-grounded recommendations, and prioritized customer intelligence.
**Habit:** Manual support, basic chat widgets, spreadsheets, and one-off agency audits.
**Anxiety:** Data privacy, incorrect recommendations, integration effort, and uncertainty about AI visibility claims.
